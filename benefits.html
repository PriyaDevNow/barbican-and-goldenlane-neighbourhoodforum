Benefits of a Neighbourhood Forum

The following are the main benefits of the Forum

Statutory Right of a Designated Forum

The Forum would have the statutory right to create a Neighbourhood Plan – this would form part of the development plan and sit alongside the City of London Corporation’s Local Plan. Decisions on planning applications would then be made using both the local plan and the neighbourhood plan, and any other material considerations. The government will provide grant funding of upto £18,000 to the designated forum for drawing up the neighbourhood plan as well as technical support

Statutory Consultee for Planning Applications

The Forum would be a statutory consultee on all planning applications presented at the City of London Corporation.

CIL Spend

The Forum would be able to influence how 25% of the revenues raised under Community Infrastructure Levy (“CIL”) by the City of London Corporation for the improvement of the local community. The CIL is imposed on each planning permission granted for new developments. In its 2021/22 Annual Report, the City of London states that it raised around £10m in the year under this levy, held a balance of £43m of unspent CIL from previous years and will potentially raise a further £42m CIL if all the planning applications approved in 2021/22 get built.
